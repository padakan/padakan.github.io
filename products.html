iCulture T-Shirt

140 THB

iCulture T-Shirt comes in green or blue, v-shaped collar, with the text “iCulture” screened on it. The symbol iCulture, which is the unofficial name for the curators, represents the identity of the people who are passionate in the museum and the culture, reflecting the contemporariness through the letter ‘i’ and signifying the cultural value through the word culture.

Pakakayor Dress

1,400 THB

Pakakayor dress signifies the beauty of simplicity and the delicacy in the decorations of the dress. The dresses are especially selected with the traditionality and originality of Pakakayor in mind.

Packaging Containers

Phor Repa Bag

140 THB

Phor Repa Bag is a handmade bag weaved from cotton specially made by the Pakakayor, the descendants of Phor Repa who was the philosopher of Pakakayor, Ban Mae Hae Tai, Pang Hin Fon Sub-district, Mae Jam District, Chiang Mai, and the role model who led a simple life living in harmonious with nature.

Luo Ban Hor Bag

290 THB

Luo Bag, from Ban Hor, Pang Hin Fon Sub-district, Mae Jam District, Chiang Mai, is a long-strapped bag weaved with white or black cotton, embroidered with a nice contrast of red and yellow fabric edge similar to Luo’s dress. Luo is an ethnic group who loves peace and harmony, they are as kind and polite as the Pakakayor.

The Broo Tha Long Sticker Rice Wicker

140 THB

The Broo Sticky Rice Wicker is a handcraft from the wisdom of thoroughly crafting the crafts which starting from selecting the most tender-skinned, fibrous, and durable bamboo, then preserving it by soaking it with water to prevent it from crumbling away. Every Sticky Rice Wicker is made with care and takes a period of time; if there is any mistake, it will get redone from the start.

Clothing Accessories

Mouse and Bird Mobile

140 THB

Mouse and Bird Mobile from Tai Dam community, Banna Pa Nad, Kao Gaew Sub-district, Chiang Kan District, Leoy, is an art artifact made from various-coloured threads delicately bound on a tiny bamboo sticks forming a house or nest of different animals.

Pakakayor Bead Necklace

290 THB

A creation crafted by the Pakakayor and the volunteers in the ‘the museum who Pid Thong Lang Phra’ volunteering activity during the 24th to the 27th of February, 2018, at Ban Mae Hae Tai, Mae Jam District, Chiang Mai. The volunteers learnt and helped the Pakakayor craft the bead necklace. The completed creation becomes one of the products sold in the museum to promote their occupations and preserve the culture of the community.

Tai Yuan Mobile is a beautiful decoration made from various-coloured threads bound on the cross-shaped bamboo sticks. It reflects the pattern of colours which is one of the unique characteristics of Tai Yuan.
