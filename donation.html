Academic Service

01

The Art of Museum Training

The training is to further the academic knowledge in the field of museum and culture to the first fundraising. It is the most successful method of earning revenue in the monetary form amongst all the fundraising projects. It is also an exposure for the museum to the other curators. Additionally, it helps expanding the connection and creating a good opportunity to share knowledge amongst the anthropological culture museum.

Exhibition, Seminar, and Fair at the Museum Expo

“Museum Expo 2016 the wonder of Thai museums”, held on the 16th to the 21st of September 2016 at Bangkok National Museum, was organized by a research group of National Museum Office, Fine Arts Department. Anthropological Culture Museum and Research Institute for Languages and Cultures of Asia also participated in the discussion forum on the issues of the teaching about museum at the present time.

Oversea Excursion

02

The International Field

The oversea excursion is the fundraising activity that is extended from the Art of Museum Training, in which the curator’s training is extended by visiting and observing the museums and cultural heritages in the international context, especially the ASEAN, countries in Asia, Singapore, Malaysia during October to November 2015, and Japan during October to November 2016.

Museum Shop

03

Origin and Purpose

The anthropological culture museum established the shop to fundraise since 2015 till the present. The museum shop sells the products on the occasions of opening up for the museum visit and discussion forum fairs and cultural festivals. The products are related to the culture and the ethnic groups, including the ethnic arts and crafts, and the products designed by the museum curators (iCulture). The museum’s goal of fundraising through the museum shop is (1) to emphasize the social engagement between the museum and the society, (2) to help developing the ethnic community through patronization of the ethnic arts and crafts creating contemporariness for the museum; which possess the beauty, meaningfulness, and the utility, aiming to cultivate the love of culture’s mentality in people, lastly, making an identity and a brand of the museum including generating revenue in the monetary form to cover the expense of the museum activity.

Volunteer Activity

04

Brainstorming

Brainstorming the fundraising activity is to evaluate the outlook, opinions, the body of knowledge, experience, and the methods of fundraising from the network of people who have completed the art of museum training. The first fundraising of the anthropological culture museum and the curators was arranged on the 21st of January 2016 at the Research Institute for Languages and Cultures of Asia, Mahidol University.

Volunteer at the local museum, Ban Na Pa Nuad

The volunteer at the local museum, Ban Na Pa Nuad, was held to aim at promoting the people to be citizens who contribute to the public good by volunteering. This formed the volunteer group who are determined to improve the local, teach knowledge and skills, as well as create an understanding from truly accessing the lifestyle of the ethnic group which leads to the empathy and acceptance in the difference of people in the society.

Volunteer at the local museum, Ban Mae Hae Tai

The volunteer at the local museum creates an opportunity for both the university or the college students and the network of people who have completed the art of museum training to step into the field, experience and learn the pakakayor lifestyle through a daily cultural routines or activities. The activities include bead embroidery, clothes weaving, trekking, fishing, cooking, sitting in a circle eating, and learning everyday conversation with the pakakayorans. Then the volunteers will collect the information using however means they would like to, and analyze those information from their understanding and perception to form a story that they want to tell which helps designing the exhibition for the Por Lae Pa museum located at the house of pakakayor philosophers who are the embodiment of the simple lifestyle.
